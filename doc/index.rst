Introduction
------------

.. automodule:: maskarray

The python module `maskarray` enables the efficient representation of
binary (on-off) process masks, as found in many EDA fields, specifically
designed with automization of ASIC layout mask data in mind.

Contributions
-------------

There are many ways you as an individual can contribute. We are happy about feature requests, bug reports and of course contributions in form of additional features. To these ends, please step by at `Github <https://github.com/EMS-TU-Ilmenau/maskarray>`_ where we organize the work on the package.
